Sanrio Inc. originated in Japan and was founded in 1976. It is owned by Tomokuni Tsuji, and thier headquarters is in Shinagawa City, Tokyo, Japan. Sanrio has seen a big boom in increased popularity in gen Z's and characters are starting to be seen once again in more stores for stationery, clothes, and accessory. Sanrio created each character designed to be able to connect with people, and each created a back story along with a personality Which is what made Sanrio's characters popular in the first place, and also because of the cute design.
