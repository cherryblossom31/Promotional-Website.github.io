My Melody was created by Sanrio in 1975, she is a white rabbit who is most known for her pink or red hood and mini cape. She was created as Hello Kitty's best friend and is depicted as an "honest and good-natured" person. In 2022 she and Cinnamoroll have become paired together in stores they will usually have a product in both Melody and Cinnamoroll designs, and in Osaka, Japan there is a My Melody-themed Cafe, like in Tokyo where they have a Cinnamoroll-themed cafe.
