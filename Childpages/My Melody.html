My Melody

My Melody was created by Sanrio in 1975, she is a white rabbit who is most known by her pink or red hood and mini cape. She was created as Hello Kittys best friend, and is depicted as a "honest and good-natured" person. In 2022 her, and Cinnamoroll have become paired together like in stores they will usually have a product in both Melody and Cinnamoroll designs, and in Osaka, Japan there is a My Melody themed Cafe, like in Tokyo where they have a Cinnamoroll themed cafe.
