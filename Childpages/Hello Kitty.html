Hello Kitty was created in 1974 by Sanrio,and was the first character of many created by the company. This character has gained a lot of popularity over the years starting in Japan then all over the world with merchendise, and clothing due to her iconic bow. Hello kitty also known by her full name Kitty white is originaly designed to be wearing overals with a red or yellow shirt underneth, but over the year Sanrio would release "special addition" versions with her being in different outfits like a Japanese kimono.
